Project Information

  • Client: iMaxeam – IBM Gold Business Partner
  • Project Date: From October 2022 to present

As Senior DevOps Engineer and Tech Lead for the MAXapps project, I designed, implemented, and maintained Jenkins CI/CD pipelines to automate the complete build and release lifecycle for mobile applications and backend services.

The pipelines cover source control validation, environment preparation, Android APK and iOS IPA build processes, artifact packaging, and secure release automation. Jenkins was also integrated with configuration management and deployment tools to ensure reliable and repeatable releases across environments.
